I've been playing badminton for quite a while now, and I've come to the conclusion that it's a great sport that provides a lot of exercise. In addition, you can play it in any weather since it's an indoor sport! My hand-eye coordination and flexibility has greatly improved, which is why this sport is not only fun, but beneficial as well! Below are some more advantages of playing badminton:

Advantages of playing badminton:

-It improves muscle strength
-It improves heart functioning
-It helps reduce stress
-It improves flexibility
-It increases your lung capacity
-It strengthens your bones
-It improves mental agility
-It helps in weight loss
-It increases your reflex action
-And many more...!

Fun Facts:

-Badminton is the fastest racquet sport in the world; shuttles can reach speeds of over 200 mph!

-There are badminton players all around the world, and badminton has become the second-most popular sport in the world behind soccer!

-The longest badminton match on record lasted a total of 124 minutes, whereas the shortest lasted only 6 minutes!

-The original name for "Badminton" was "Shuttlecock" and "Battledore"!

-When badminton made it's Olympic debut at the 1992 games, it was seen on television by over 1.1 billion people!
