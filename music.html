Ever since I started learning piano at the age of 6, It's been a very big part of my life and is one of my go-to hobbies. Playing the piano helps me rewind when I'm stressed, and it's also a great way to express your creativity using music. After finally completing my Level 10 piano practical exam back in the summer, I can't help but feel accomplished at the fact that all my hard work has finally paid off!

Here are some benefits to playing the piano and why you should try it:

-It helps relieve stress
-It improves your multitasking skills and concentration skills
-It stimulates the brain and primes it for other forms of communication
-It improves dexterity and hand-eye coordination
-It can improve your overall aural awareness
-And many more...!

Playing the piano has many benefits, which is why I strongly encourage you to try it if you haven't already!

-Waltz in C-sharp minor, Op.64 No.2 by Frédéric Chopin

-Liebestraume, S541/R211: No.3: Nocturne in A-Flat Major by Franz Liszt

-Waltz No.19 in A Minor, Op. Posth. by Frédéric Chopin

-Sonata No.28 in A Major, Op.101 by Ludwig van Beethoven
