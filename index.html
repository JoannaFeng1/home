Welcome to my webpage about me, Joanna! I'm 14 years old and currently attending Victoria Park C.I. in Toronto, Ontario. The purpose of this webpage is for you to get to know my interests and hobbies, as well as more general information about who I am.

As shown in the navigation bar, there are 3 topics this website will be covering about me: Music, Drawing and Badminton. The first one, Music, will tell you about one of my favourite hobbies: playing the piano. The second one, Drawing, will talk about my love for drawing and why it's very beneficial. And last but not least, Badminton, which talks about why I enjoy playing badminton as a hobby and some fun facts about the sport.



Thanks for taking the time to explore this website and I hope you enjoy it!
